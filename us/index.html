what is dla?

Hey there, welcome to dumbluckartsl a non professional indie development studio making games for the sake of it. Currently we're a small team just working towards getting a few projects done to show our moms. Nothing special here, just a bunch of dudes trying to make new media.

We want you to have fun.

Dla stands by our creations, during our development period everything is free as we deem so. We dont want you paying for things that arent finished.

Where are all these creations?

Why great question, all of our games, art, and music can be found right here on our website. This is the first place we tend to update because its just so easy for us. We have a dedicated website developer on staff and what else are we paying him for.
